> cat principles.txt

                

                    

                        
usable

                        
If it needs an explanation, it needs a redesign. Clarity is not a feature — it's the
                            baseline.

                    

                    

                        
intentional

                        
Every pixel earns its place. Decoration that doesn't communicate is just debt with a drop
                            shadow.

                    

                    

                        
evidence-based

                        
Strong opinions, loosely held, heavily tested. Intuition gets you to a hypothesis. Research
                            makes it a decision.

                    

                    

                        
shipped

                        
Perfect is the enemy of in-production. I build to learn, iterate fast, and measure everything
                            that matters.
