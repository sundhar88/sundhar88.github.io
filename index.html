my principles.txt

usable

If it needs an explanation, it needs a redesign. Clarity is not a feature — it's the baseline.

intentional

Every pixel earns its place. Decoration that doesn't communicate is just debt.

evidence-based

Strong opinions, loosely held, heavily tested. Research makes it a decision.

shipped

Perfect is the enemy of in-production. I build to learn, iterate fast.
